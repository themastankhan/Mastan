Functional Consultant - Dynamic 365 CRM -Marketing/Sales/Customer Service

Position: Functional Consultant - Dynamic 365 CRM -Marketing/Sales/Customer Service

Location: Hyderabad, Telangana

Experience Required: 7-8 Years

No of Positions: 03

 

Responsibilities:

Solution Design and Implementation:

➢ Collaborate with clients to understand their Marketing/Sales/Customer Service requirements and design solutions using Dynamics 365 CRM for Marketing/Sales/Customer Service.

➢ Implement and configure Dynamics 365 CRM Marketing/Sales/Customer Service modules to meet client specifications.

➢ Customize and extend the system to address unique business needs.

 

Business Process Analysis:

➢ Conduct thorough analysis of Marketing/Sales/Customer Service business processes and identify areas fo improvement and optimization.

➢ Work closely with business stakeholders to gather and document functional requirements.

 

Requirements Gathering:

➢ Engage with clients to understand their Marketing/Sales/Customer Service goals and objectives.

➢ Elicit and document detailed functional and technical requirements for CRM Marketing/Sales/Customer Service processes.

 

Dynamics 365 CRM Configuration:

➢ Configure and customize Dynamics 365 CRM Marketing/Sales/Customer Service modules based on business requirements.

➢ Implement and manage Marketing/Sales/Customer Service automation, lead management, and campaign management functionalities.

 

Integration:

➢ Collaborate with technical teams to design and implement integrations between Dynamics 365 CRM Marketing/Sales/Customer Service and other systems.

➢ Ensure seamless data flow and synchronization between CRM and Marketing/Sales/Customer Service tools.

Training and Documentation:

➢ Conduct training sessions for end-users on the use of Dynamics 365 CRM Marketing/Sales/Customer Service features.

➢ Create and maintain detailed documentation for configurations, customizations, and processes.

 

Testing and Quality Assurance:

➢ Develop and execute test plans to ensure the functionality, usability, and performance of Dynamics 365 CRM Marketing/Sales/Customer Service modules.

➢ Collaborate with testing teams to validate integrations and data migrations.

 

Client Support:

➢ Provide ongoing support to clients, addressing inquiries, issues, and enhancement requests related to Dynamics 365 CRM Marketing/Sales/Customer Service.

➢ Work closely with the support team to resolve incidents and ensure client satisfaction.

 

Qualifications:

➢ Bachelor’s degree in Business, Information Technology, or related field.

➢ Proven experience as a functional consultant with a focus on Dynamics 365 CRM Marketing/Sales/Customer Service.

➢ In-depth knowledge of Dynamics 365 CRM Marketing/Sales/Customer Service modules and capabilities.

➢ Experience in configuring and customizing Dynamics 365 CRM to meet business requirements.

➢ Familiarity with marketing automation tools and integration concepts.

➢ Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

➢ Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.

➢ Relevant certifications in Dynamics 365 CRM will be a plus
