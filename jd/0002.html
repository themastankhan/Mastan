CRM Functional Consultant - Marketing

Role:CRM Functional Consultant - Marketing
Location: Hyderabad
Expereince: 3-5 Years
No of Positions: 03

Responsibilities:
Solution Design and Implementation:
➢ Collaborate with clients to understand their marketing requirements and design solutions using CRM for Marketing.
➢ Implement and configure CRM Marketing modules to meet client specifications.
➢ Customize and extend the system to address unique business needs.

Business Process Analysis:
➢ Conduct thorough analysis of marketing business processes and identify areas for improvement and optimization.
➢ Work closely with business stakeholders to gather and document functional requirements.

Requirements Gathering:
➢ Engage with clients to understand their marketing goals and objectives.
➢ Elicit and document detailed functional and technical requirements for CRM marketing processes.

CRM Configuration:
➢ Configure and customize CRM Marketing modules based on business
requirements.
➢ Implement and manage marketing automation, lead management, and campaign management functionalities.

Integration:
➢ Collaborate with technical teams to design and implement integrations between CRM Marketing and other systems.
➢ Ensure seamless data flow and synchronization between CRM and marketing tools.
Training and Documentation:
➢ Conduct training sessions for end-users on the use of CRM Marketing features.
➢ Create and maintain detailed documentation for configurations, customizations, and processes.

Testing and Quality Assurance:
➢ Develop and execute test plans to ensure the functionality, usability, and performance of CRM Marketing modules.
➢ Collaborate with testing teams to validate integrations and data migrations

Client Support:
➢ Provide ongoing support to clients, addressing inquiries, issues, and enhancement requests related to CRM Marketing.
➢ Work closely with the support team to resolve incidents and ensure client satisfaction.

Qualifications:
➢ Bachelor’s degree in Business, Information Technology, or related field.
➢ Proven experience as a functional consultant with a focus on CRM Marketing.
➢ In-depth knowledge of CRM Marketing modules and capabilities.
➢ Strong understanding of marketing processes, lead management, and campaign management.
➢ Experience in configuring and customizing CRM to meet business requirements.
➢ Familiarity with marketing automation tools and integration concepts.
➢ Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
➢ Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
➢ Relevant certifications in CRM will be a plus.
