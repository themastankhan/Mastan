Civil Design Engineer - Renewable Energy

Role: Civil Design Engineer - Renewable Energy

Job ID: 0033

Industry: Renewable Energy

Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra

Experience Required: 6 - 10 Years

Education: BE / B.Tech (Civil)


The Design Engineer (Civil) in the renewable energy business is responsible for designing and planning civil engineering aspects of renewable energy projects, ensuring the safe, efficient, and sustainable development of renewable energy facilities. This role involves working closely with cross-functional teams, including environmental specialists, project managers, and construction teams, to deliver high-quality civil engineering designs that comply with industry standards and regulations.


Job Context & Major Challenges:

India has been rapidly expanding its renewable energy sector, aiming to meet its energy demands sustainably and reduce its dependence on fossil fuels. As a Design Engineer (Civil) working in the renewable energy business, you would be part of a dynamic and evolving team that offers exciting opportunities to contribute to the exponential growth journey of the business and nation's energy transition. Your role would involve designing and planning civil engineering aspects of various renewable energy projects, such as solar power plants, wind farms, floater projects etc. You'll collaborate with multidisciplinary teams and work on projects of varying scales.


Principal Accountabilities:

  • Site Assessment and Feasibility Studies
    • Conduct site assessments and feasibility studies to evaluate the suitability of locations for renewable energy projects.
    • Analyze geotechnical data, topographic surveys, and environmental impact assessments to identify potential challenges and opportunities related to civil engineering design.
  • Civil Engineering Design
    • Develop detailed civil engineering designs for renewable energy projects, including solar farms, wind farms, floaters, and other facilities.
    • Prepare site layouts, grading plans, drainage systems, foundation designs, and road infrastructure to support renewable energy installations.
    • Ensure that civil designs align with project objectives, local regulations, and environmental considerations.
  • Collaboration and Coordination
    • Collaborate with the project development team, environmental experts, and other stakeholders to integrate civil engineering requirements into the overall project plan.
    • Coordinate with external consultants, contractors, and suppliers to obtain necessary inputs and ensure timely project delivery.
  • Permitting and Compliance
    • Assist in obtaining necessary permits and approvals for civil engineering aspects of renewable energy projects.
    • Ensure compliance with local, state, and central regulations and environmental standards throughout the design process.
  • Technical Evaluation
    • Evaluate the technical aspects of civil engineering proposals from vendors and subcontractors to ensure adherence to project requirements and quality standards.
    • Conduct technical reviews and provide recommendations to support procurement decisions.
  • Quality Assurance and Risk Management
    • Implement quality assurance processes to verify the accuracy and completeness of civil engineering designs.
    • Identify potential risks related to civil engineering aspects and develop mitigation strategies.
    • Provide a design to safeguard our company's safety standards.
  • Sustainability and Innovation
    • Integrate sustainable design principles and innovative technologies to enhance the performance and efficiency of renewable energy projects.
    • Stay updated on industry best practices, new technologies and advancements in civil engineering design for renewable energy applications.

Work Mode: Work From Office

Notice Period: 30 Days

Mode of Interview: Telephonic and Face to Face
