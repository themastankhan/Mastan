Job Description

Job Responsibilities and Duties:

Sales Target Achievement:

  • Proactively sell Milk and Milk Products to Businesses or HoReCa (Hotel, Restaurant, and Catering) establishments.
  • Meet and exceed assigned sales targets.


Reporting and Communication:

  • Provide daily sales reports to the Branch Manager, highlighting achievements and challenges.
  • Maintain regular communication with the management team regarding market trends and customer feedback.


Customer Relationship Management:

  • Cultivate and nurture relationships with existing customers through in-person meetings, telephone calls, and emails.
  • Respond promptly to incoming inquiries via email and phone, addressing customer needs and concerns.


Market Analysis:

  • Gather valuable market and customer information to identify opportunities and potential challenges.
  • Stay informed about industry trends and competitor activities


Product Knowledge and Promotion:

  • Stay updated on product developments and upcoming releases.
  • Advise customers on forthcoming product developments and discuss special promotions.


Eligibility requirements:

  • Graduate with a preference for computer literacy (MS Office skills).
  • Minimum 2 years of experience in a related field, preferably in the Food & Dairy Industry.
  • Possession of a valid Driving License and knowledge of local routes.


Maximum age limit: 39 years.

Previous Product Experience:

  • Fresh milk and Dairy products
  • Any FMCG edible products
