CRM Functional Consultant - Field Service

Position:CRM Functional Consultant - Field Service
Location: Hyderabad
Experience: 3-5 Years
No of Positions: 03

Overview:
We are seeking a highly skilled Functional Consultant with expertise in CRM, specifically in
the Field Service module. The ideal candidate will play a key role in implementing and customizing
CRM solutions for our clients, with a focus on optimizing field service operations.

Responsibilities:
Requirement Analysis:
➢ Collaborate with clients to gather and analyze business requirements related to field service
operations.
➢ Conduct workshops and interviews to understand current business processes and pain points.
Solution Design:
➢ Design and propose effective solutions based on CRM, specifically focusing on the
Field Service module.
➢ Create functional specifications and documentation for the proposed solutions.
Configuration and Customization:
➢ Configure and customize CRM to meet the client's requirements.
➢ Implement and manage field service processes, including work order management, scheduling,
resource management, and customer communication.

Integration:
➢ Work with technical teams to design and implement integrations between CRM
and other business systems (ERP, HR, etc.).
➢ Ensure seamless data flow and integration to support end-to-end business processes.

Testing:
➢ Develop and execute test plans to ensure the quality and reliability of implemented solutions.
➢ Work closely with testing teams and end-users to validate functionality.
Training and Support:
➢ Conduct training sessions for end-users and provide ongoing support.
➢ Address and resolve issues reported by end-users, ensuring customer satisfaction.

Documentation:
➢ Maintain detailed documentation of configurations, customizations, and solutions implemented.
➢ Create user guides and training materials.
Best Practices and Innovation:
➢ Stay updated on the latest features and updates in CRM.
➢ Provide insights and recommendations for continuous improvement and innovation.

Qualifications:
➢ Bachelor's degree in a relevant field (Computer Science, Business, etc.).
➢ Proven experience as a Functional Consultant with a focus on CRM.
➢ In-depth knowledge of CRM, particularly the Field Service module.
➢ Strong understanding of field service operations and best practices.
➢ Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
➢ Ability to work collaboratively in a team and independently.
➢ Certifications in CRM are a plus.
