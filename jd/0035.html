Graphic Designer

We are hiring Graphic Designers in Vadodara, Gujarat, we are looking for candidates who have 1-7 years of experience.

Role: Graphic Designer

Job ID: 0035

Location: Vadodara, Gujarat

Experience Required: 1-7 Years

Gender: Male Candidates Only

We're looking for a talented graphic designer who can take concepts and ideas and create visual representations, in both print and electronic media. The ideal candidate will have expert knowledge of current design software and be skilled in every step of the design process, from concept to final deliverable. Collaborating with multiple teams across the company, the graphic designer should be able to take written ideas and convert them into a design that connects. The successful candidate will have a thorough understanding of branding and marketing, and be able to find the right style and layout for every project.


Objectives of this role

  • Work on a wide range of projects and media, using various software programs to visualise and develop innovative graphic designs that meet business goals
  • Obtain input from managers to ensure that designs meet organisational standards and brand expectations, express ideas accurately, and represent the company or client appropriately
  • Work independently as well as cooperatively with team leaders to meet deadlines
  • Examine existing processes, identify flaws, and create solutions that improve design capabilities
  • Develop concepts with software, and execute original content by determining the ideal usage of color, text, font style, imagery, and layout
  • Manage the design and uploading process for all project materials

Required skills and qualifications

  • Exceptional creativity and innovative design skills
  • One or more years of experience with design software, including Illustrator & Photoshop
  • Must be good in Reading writing and Speaking English
  • Organisational and time-management skills for meeting deadlines in a fast-paced environment
  • Desire to continue building skill set with training
  • Should be comfortable working in US Shift (6:30 pm to 4:30 am IST)

Interview Process:

  • Interview Process: 2 rounds of interviews, 1 technical test, 1 HR round For local candidates (everything at the office)
  • HR round of interview
  • Production round of interview
  • Technical test
  • HR Round - salary discussion

For remote candidates:

  • HR round of interview (over cell phone)
  • Production round of interview (Virtual over Zoom or Google Meet)
  • Technical test (online)
  • HR Round - salary discussion (over cell phone)

Compensation Details

  • Joining Bonus For Immediate Joiners Who Are Currently Employed
