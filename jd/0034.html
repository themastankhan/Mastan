Senior Business Development Executive (Property/Construction Technology)

Role: Senior Business Development Executive

Industry: Property/Construction Technology

Job ID: 0034

Location: Kuvempu Nagar, Bengaluru

Experience Required: 3 - 5 Years

Education: Any Graduate

Salary: 5 - 7.5 L.P.A


Job Summary:
To achieve our ambitious goal, we are looking for a Sr. Business Development Executive (BDE) who is proactive in nature and works in a pre-sales and sales role, and generates leads on his/her own using different methods (B2B portals, emails, cold calls) from the enterprise customer segment.


Opportunity:
In this role, you will be responsible for driving new sales and the growth of our company in the enterprise segment. The person in this role will pioneer some of our biggest deals by landing new logos and formalizing enterprise-wide relationships with companies. You will gain knowledge and experience in the new-age industry of SaaS and cloud.


Impact:
As a Senior Business Development Executive, you will work with sales and leadership teams to unlock new relationships at large organizations and evolve our B2B revenue engine. You’ll also have support from our growing Sales, Product, Marketing, Customer Success, and Engineering teams to maximize the value and success of your efforts. Over the next few years, our company seeks to establish and grow a team. As a pioneering member of that team, you’ll have opportunities to manage future AEs, if that career path interests you.


Responsibilities:

  • Identify potential customer targets from enterprise/corporate segments.
  • Research key contacts of Architects, MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) Consultants, and PMC (Project Management Consultant) for Commercial Real Estate projects
  • Closely working with various stakeholders (horizontally & vertically) on the new project requirements and getting specifications incorporated
  • Collaborate with the internal sales team & external partner stakeholders in the sales and specification journey
  • Apply consultative sales skills to build relationships with stakeholders
  • Close deals, acquire new customers, generate revenue

We’re looking for someone who:

  • Is passionate about sales and technology products
  • Has proven experience of 3-5 years in the Building material industry and promoting, specifying, and selling products/solutions to various stakeholders for the architects, MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) consultants and PMC (Project Management Consultant) community
  • Has experience of working with consultants, architects, design & build, HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) consultants, lighting consultants, PMC (Project Management Consultant) and Specify our products, and solutions, getting necessary approvals.
  • Team player, with excellent oral & written communication, and presentation skills and must be able to articulate technology & product positioning to both business & technical users.
  • Has knowledge of the technical specifications, procurement process, decision-making criteria, processes used in the industry etc
  • Is extremely motivated to succeed and is a problem solver
  • Knowledge of complex decision-making groups (or ability to grasp this quickly)
  • Proven track record of planning sales pipeline
  • Well organized, analytical, detail-oriented and quality-minded, able to eliminate sales obstacles through creative and adaptive approaches.

Good to have:

  • Worked on CRM Tools
  • Worked on B2B portals to generate leads
  • Education in BBA/BCA/Engineering degree, or equivalent

Benefits:

  • Medical Insurance
  • Travel allowance
  • Employee Stock Options (ESOP)
  • Access to the e-learning platform
  • Incentive on sales closure

Others:

  • Travel - Local, Intercity

Interview process:
HR intro call -> Head of sales intro call -> 1 hour F2F/online interview with Head of sales and CEO -> Presentation round -> Final discussion
